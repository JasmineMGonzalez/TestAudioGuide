There are 9 experiences within You:Matter and each of these is a section in this guide.

You:Matter is an immersive digital art experience with some access considerations.

Swipe to read more

To Navigate the Guide: Look out for the headphones symbol in the exhibition and then hit the number you’re in front of to begin.

Press the menu icon at the top right of the screen to change stops.

Use the buttons at the bottom of the screen to move to the next or previous stop.

Access Considerations:

The light and contrast levels throughout the gallery are low

There are ambient soundtracks specific to each area

Transcripts to spoken content are provided in this guide

Audio of printed content is provided in this guide

The first and final experiences feature reflective flooring and walls...

Sensors to interact with We Live In An Ocean Of Air and Tree of Life are mounted in the middle of the floor on dark coloured concrete plinths

Head in the Clouds features two large suspended clouds at head height, visitors can duck inside to hear the content and have a photo opp.

There are floor markers for cane users here.

The sensors to interact with Sunlight Under My Skin are on a plinth in the centre of the floor.

Place your hand on one of the pillars to start the interaction.

Seating is provided in We Live In An Ocean Of Air and Tree of Life
